#hackPHX

A hackathon is an event that brings together engineers, designers, developers, entrepreneurs anyone with good ideas into the same space to get back to MAKING things. Our challenges will include software, hardware, cryptography, charity and who knows what else!

Your quest, should you choose to accept is .. Arduino!

You'll have ~12 hours to build whatever you want using an Arduino, our secret ingredient, the hackerspace's resources, and a small budget to buy additional stuff from Walmart, Radioshack, Craigslist, etc. Near the end of the ~12 hour period judges will pick their favorite, and participants will award the Jury prize, but we promise everyone will go home with hardware.
